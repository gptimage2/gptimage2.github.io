FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are GPT Image 2 prompts?

GPT Image 2 prompts are text instructions that describe the image you want: subject, scene, style, camera angle, lighting, layout, text, colors, and constraints. A useful prompt reads like a compact creative brief rather than a single vague sentence.

How do I write better GPT Image 2 prompts?

Start with the goal, then add the subject, visual style, composition, lighting, aspect ratio, and any text that must appear. For better GPT Image 2 prompt examples, quote exact words, limit clutter, and tell the generator what should stay readable or realistic.

Can GPT Image 2 render text accurately?

Text rendering depends on the image workflow being used, the length of the phrase, and the complexity of the layout. Short quoted text, clear hierarchy, strong contrast, and a direct instruction such as "keep all words legible" usually gives a cleaner starting point.

What are the best GPT Image 2 prompts for posters?

The best GPT Image 2 prompts for posters define the headline, subtitle, event or product context, typography style, background, spacing, and color contrast. Keep the copy short and make the prompt describe hierarchy, not just mood.
